Welcome to the Computational Neuroscience Club. Computational Neuroscience (also called theoretical neuroscience) is the field of describing the functions of the brain using mathematical and computational models. We will be covering topics such as:

  • Neurobiology
  • Basics of Information Theory
  • Neural Networks
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Brain-Machine Interfaces
  • Brain Imaging Techniques
  • Notable Past Experiments
  • Prerequisite Math Concepts
